Guided White Beach Journey with Stay at Beach View Auberge

2 Persons

Price: 1000 DH

BOOK YOUR JOURNEY

Set out on the Journey to the White Beach for 2 persons, staying in a welcoming beach view auberge with spectacular views of the Atlantic Ocean. This journey combines relaxation, exploration, and authentic Moroccan coastal culture, perfect for couples or friends seeking a unique and memorable adventure.

During this journey, you will enjoy a delicious lunch in Guelmim and be accompanied by a knowledgeable guide who will provide insights into the region’s history, culture, and hidden gems. Explore the pristine white sands and scenic coastline, creating unforgettable moments along Morocco’s Atlantic shores.

Your stay includes breakfast and dinner daily at the auberge, featuring fresh local ingredients and traditional Moroccan flavors. The room offers a private bathroom, balcony, and terrace with panoramic sea views, perfect for relaxing after a day of coastal exploration. Guests can also enjoy a fully equipped kitchen, coffee machine, outdoor dining space, and cozy seating area for maximum comfort.

This journey is designed for both adventure and relaxation. Enjoy mornings with the gentle sea breeze, afternoons exploring Guelmim and the white beach, and evenings savoring Moroccan cuisine while taking in the breathtaking ocean views. It’s an immersive coastal experience combining nature, culture, and comfort.
