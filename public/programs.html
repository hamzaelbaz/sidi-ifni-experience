FAQS

Common questions
What experiences does Sidi Ifni Experiences offer? +

We provide a full Sidi Ifni experience that includes outdoor activities like beach trekking, mountain trips, camel rides, paragliding, and fishing — all combined with comfortable stays at our ocean-view auberge.

Do you offer accommodation? +

Yes! You can stay at our traditional auberge located near Sidi Ifni Beach. It offers cozy rooms, ocean views, and easy access to all our activities, creating a complete and relaxing getaway.

Are your activities suitable for families and groups? +

Yes, our activities are perfect for families, couples, and groups. We adapt each experience to different ages and comfort levels to ensure everyone enjoys their time at Sidi Ifni.

Do you provide guides and equipment? +

All our experiences include professional local guides, and we provide the necessary equipment and safety gear for activities such as paragliding, fishing, or beach camping.

How do I get to Sidi Ifni Experiences? +

Sidi Ifni is located near Sidi Ifni, about 2.5 hours from Agadir. We can assist with transportation arrangements upon request to make your visit smooth and stress-free.

What should I bring with me? +

We recommend bringing comfortable shoes, sunscreen, a hat, and a camera. For certain activities, we’ll share additional details before your arrival.

What is your cancellation policy? +

You can cancel up to 48 hours before your stay or activity for a full refund. For last-minute changes, please contact us — we always try to be flexible for our guests.

When is the best time to visit Sidi Ifni? +

The best time to visit Sidi Ifni is from April to October, when the weather is warm and perfect for outdoor adventures and beach activities.
