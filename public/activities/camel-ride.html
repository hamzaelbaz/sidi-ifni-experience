Camel Riding Adventure

Half-day beach rides

Private or group tours

BOOK YOUR CAMEL RIDE

Discover Morocco’s coastal magic with our camel ride in Legzira, starting from our auberge or directly from the golden beach. Ride peacefully along the Atlantic shore and make your way to the iconic red arch of Legzira — a breathtaking natural wonder shaped by wind and sea over centuries.

Accompanied by friendly local guides, you’ll experience the gentle rhythm of the camels while admiring the dramatic cliffs, endless ocean views, and wild beauty of the coastline. The sound of waves, the sea breeze, and the soft light of Legzira create the perfect atmosphere for an unforgettable moment.

Along the way, we’ll pause to take photos and enjoy a traditional Moroccan mint tea served right by the beach. Your guide will share fascinating stories about Legzira’s history, local traditions, and the coastal life of Sidi Ifni.

Whether you’re looking for a romantic experience, a peaceful solo escape, or a fun family activity, this camel ride in Legzira is the perfect way to connect with nature and discover one of Morocco’s most magical beaches.

DURATION

Approx. 1–2 hours (half-day option)

MEETING POINT

Departure from our Auberge or Legzira Beach

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Guided camel ride to Legzira arches
  • Local and friendly guides
  • Traditional mint tea stop
  • Photo opportunities

NOTES

Wear light clothes and a hat. Suitable for all ages. Available even if you don’t stay at the auberge.
