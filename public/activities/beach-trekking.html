BOOK YOUR BEACH TREK

Experience the beauty of Morocco’s southern coast with our beach trekking tour from Legzira to Sidi Ifni. Starting from the famous red arches of Legzira, this scenic coastal hike takes you along golden beaches, dramatic cliffs, and peaceful ocean paths toward the charming town of Sidi Ifni.

The trek lasts about 2 hours and is perfect for those who wish to explore the wild beauty of the Atlantic coastline at their own pace. Whether you stay with us at our auberge in Legzira or elsewhere, you can easily join the adventure — the departure always begins from Legzira Beach.

Along the way, enjoy the sound of the waves, seabirds gliding above, and breathtaking panoramic views that blend ocean and desert landscapes. Your local guide will lead you through hidden trails, fishing spots, and secret viewpoints that few visitors ever see.

During the break, relax by the sea and enjoy traditional Moroccan mint tea while soaking in the tranquility of the surroundings. This beach trek from Legzira to Sidi Ifni is ideal for all levels — a mix of adventure, culture, and pure coastal serenity you won’t forget.

DURATION

Approx. 2 hours (half-day option)

MEETING POINT

Departure from Legzira Beach (our auberge or nearby location)

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Guided beach trek from Legzira to Sidi Ifni
  • Local and experienced guides
  • Traditional Moroccan tea break
  • Private or small group options

NOTES

Wear comfortable shoes and light clothing. Bring sunscreen, a hat, and water. Suitable for all ages and fitness levels. Departure available even if you don’t stay at our auberge.
