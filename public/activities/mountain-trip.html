Mountain Trekking & Picnic

Full-day experience

Includes picnic lunch

BOOK YOUR TREK

Escape the coastline and explore Morocco’s breathtaking mountain scenery with our mountain trekking trip from Legzira or Sidi Ifni. This full-day adventure combines hiking, nature, and relaxation — perfect for travelers looking to experience authentic Berber landscapes.

Starting from our auberge in Legzira or another nearby point, you’ll ascend scenic trails through valleys and rocky hills, guided by locals who know the terrain and culture. The trek leads you to panoramic viewpoints overlooking the Atlantic and the surrounding mountains.

Around midday, we stop for a traditional Moroccan picnic in nature — fresh bread, tajine, mint tea, and seasonal fruits — all enjoyed under the open sky. You’ll have time to rest, take photos, and connect with the calm rhythm of rural Morocco.

This mountain trip from Legzira is ideal for those seeking a peaceful yet adventurous escape — a unique mix of exercise, culture, and serenity far from the crowds.

DURATION

Full day (approx. 6–7 hours)

MEETING POINT

Departure from our Auberge or Legzira area

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • Guided mountain hike
  • Traditional Moroccan picnic
  • Local guide and transfers
  • Photo and rest stops

NOTES

Wear hiking shoes and bring water. Moderate fitness required. Available for private groups or individuals.

Unforgettable Experiences

Discover the beauty of Morocco’s southern coast with Sidi Ifni Experience. From peaceful camel rides in Legzira to adventurous beach treks, mountain trips with picnic, and camping nights under the stars at Berj, each activity is designed to connect you with nature and local culture. Enjoy fishing experiences along the Atlantic and surfing sessions in crystal-clear waves — every moment is crafted for travelers who seek authenticity, adventure, and Moroccan hospitality.
