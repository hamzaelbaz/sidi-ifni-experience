Fishing Experience in Sidi Ifni

2 Persons

Price: 600 DH

BOOK YOUR FISHING TRIP

Join our unique 2-hour fishing experience in Sidi Ifni, designed for travelers who want to learn authentic coastal techniques used by local fishermen. This hands-on activity is perfect for beginners and curious adventurers looking to connect with Morocco’s maritime culture.

During this session, you will learn how to catch Octopus and Sea oysters using traditional methods passed down through generations. Your local guide will take you to the best nearby coastal spots, where you’ll practice real skills in a safe and supportive environment.

This short but rich experience is ideal for anyone interested in understanding the daily life of Sidi Ifni’s fishermen while enjoying the fresh ocean air and dramatic Atlantic coastline.

Whether you're traveling solo or simply seeking a cultural activity, this fishing lesson offers a memorable and authentic glimpse into the coastal traditions of Sidi Ifni.

DURATION

2 hours

MEETING POINT

Sidi Ifni coastline

WHAT'S INCLUDED

  • All fishing tools and equipment
  • Guidance from a local expert
  • Techniques for catching Octopus and Sea oysters
  • Safety instructions and on-site assistance

NOTES

Suitable for all levels. Wear comfortable clothes and bring sun protection. This activity is designed for 1 person and costs 600 DH.

Enjoy fishing experiences along the Atlantic and surfing sessions in crystal-clear waves — every moment is crafted for travelers who seek authenticity, adventure, and Moroccan hospitality.
